# HTML Section Map

This is the copy structure derived from the landing page template comments. Do not edit the HTML. Use this to format the `.txt` output.

## hero

- goal: concise overview
- tone: executive-friendly, confident, high-level
- fields: headline, body

## perspective

- goal: West Monroe point of view on the client situation
- tone: strategic, insight-driven, not promotional
- fields: headline, body

## issues

- goal: define the client’s current problems, constraints, and roadblocks
- tone: client-centered
- fields: headline, body

## why-west-monroe

- goal: why we are the right partner for this work
- tone: specific, not generic firm marketing
- fields: headline, body

## value

- goal: expected outcomes and impact
- tone: measurable, tangible, business-led
- fields: section headline, eyebrow, title, body, stat value, stat copy

## approach

- goal: explain how we will solve the problem
- tone: practical, clear, easy to follow
- fields: headline, body, step 1 title/body, step 2 title/body, step 3 title/body

## timeline

- goal: present realistic pacing and major milestones
- tone: concrete and aligned to the approach
- fields:
  - section headline
  - section body
  - 4 phase tab labels
  - 4 phase titles
  - 4 phase summaries
  - 4 deliverables bodies
  - 4 deliverables lists

## team

- goal: show the team structure and why each role matters
- tone: relevant to the client and the work
- fields: headline, body plus 6 team cards with name, role, bio, learn-more link

## related-articles

- goal: reinforce credibility with thought leadership or press
- tone: supportive proof, not repetitive
- fields: headline, intro body plus 4 article rows with source, title, url

## case-studies

- goal: showcase relevant past work
- tone: proof-oriented and close to the client’s challenge
- fields: section headline, eyebrow, title, body, stat 1 value/copy, stat 2 value/copy

## testimonials

- goal: validate trust and execution
- tone: aligned to the expected value
- fields: headline, intro body plus 4 testimonial quote/source pairs

## pricing

- goal: explain pricing structure, scope assumptions, and cost organization
- tone: clear, transparent, professional
- fields: section headline, eyebrow, price, body, assumptions, scope notes

## fixed text

These can stay as-is in the HTML and do not need to be rewritten unless the user explicitly asks:

- confidentiality provision
- strict confidentiality paragraph

## structural constraint

Visible headlines may be rewritten to fit the proposal copy. Do not rename section keys, nav targets, div names, class names, or HTML ids.
